# SyGMA Workflow: Outputs and Artifacts

This page describes the outputs and artifacts generated by the SyGMA workflow.

## Primary Outputs

### Generated Metabolites

**Metabolite Structures:**
- Chemical structures of all generated metabolites
- SMILES representations
- 3D coordinates (if available)
- Stereochemical information

**Metabolite Properties:**
- Molecular weight and formula
- ADMET properties (Absorption, Distribution, Metabolism, Excretion, Toxicity)
- Biological activities
- Toxicity predictions

### Metabolic Pathways

**Transformation Pathways:**
- Step-by-step metabolic transformations
- Reaction mechanisms and intermediates
- Enzyme information and cofactors
- Reaction conditions and rates

**Pathway Visualization:**
- Interactive metabolic pathway diagrams
- Network graphs of transformations
- Hierarchical pathway trees
- Custom pathway layouts

## Visualization Artifacts

### Metabolic Pathway Diagrams
- **Interactive Networks**: Clickable pathway diagrams
- **Step-by-Step Transformations**: Detailed reaction sequences

- **Property Distributions**: Histograms of metabolite properties
